The First Session Is An Assessment & Discovery Session

I look forward to meeting to talk about Life Coaching, your situation, and whether or not both of us feel a potential Life Coach relationship would be helpful to you for moving forward with your life.

Learn More

Standard Coaching Session Outline:

  1. Greeting and casual catch-up

  2. Validation of client goals and any changes

  3. Update from client on prior action items

  4. Client defines topics to talk about that day and what success for the session will look like

  5. Discovery conversation: describe, picture, options, obstacles, etc.

  6. Client defines action to take for next session

  7. Coach asks for anything else to be covered in the session
