What is a Life Coach

More

Life Coach is...

  • Is a partner for a period of time to come alongside you to help you get to where you would like to go.
  • Is more focused on your growth and success than you are.
  • Listens and believes in you, your value, and your potential in life (Believes in you before you believe in yourself)
  • Helps you discover your potential and helps you move forward with it
  • Helps you see and feel your dreams and then helps you practically move toward them.
Next

Life Coaches Can Be of Great Service to People That:

  • Invest in themselves.
  • Take responsibility for their lives.
  • Work hard for results every week.
  • Are successful and yet feel unfulfilled or are dissatisfied with some aspect of their life
    • Feel stuck or frozen in life, and want to move on or through a situation and feel good about their life contribution and progress.
Next

A Life Coach Can Help You:

  • Become a stronger person.

  • Go somewhere you have not been able to get to on your own.

  • Get results faster.

  • Re-ignite your hope, drive, and belief in yourself, your future, and your dreams.

Next

A Life Coach Is Not:

A person who focuses on addressing past issues or that gives you advice. Not like a: teacher, trainer, facilitator, counselor, therapist, consultant, advisor, mentor, or good friend.
