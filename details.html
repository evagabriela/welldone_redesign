What A Life Coach Experience Looks Like:

  • We first, conduct a short introduction/question and answer call:
  1. We have a Relationship Discovery session to better understand the benefits of having a Life Coach and the requirements for the partnership.
  2. We decide together if this is a partnership they want to pursue.
  • When there is a decision to proceed:
  1. We complete a Life Coach - Client Relationship Agreement to establish well-defined commitments and boundaries for the relationship.
  2. The Agreement is discussed, completed, and signed by both the client and coach during the Discovery session.
  3. An initial life assessment process is conducted.
  4. The client establishes a target goal with the support of the coach.
  5. The client completes a Life Assessment Wheel to be reviewed together at the next session.
  • Our meetings:
  1. The client and Coach meet regularly (normally bi-weekly) for several months by phone, Skype, or in person.
  2. The sessions are client-focused, anticipating that at least 80% of the conversation comes from the client, stemming from questions asked by the coach.
  3. The client drives each of these sessions: clearly describing the vision or goal, providing an update on action items, defining the agenda for each session, and defining the desired action items for the next session.
  4. Sessions focus on better defining the details of a goal or dream, identifying obstacles and options, and then on defining and taking action steps between sessions.
  5. The coaching relationship may end at the end of the agreed time in the Coach – Client Agreement, or before, if either the client or the coach feels the partnership is not benefiting the client.
  6. A coaching experience assessment is completed to evaluate the value received by the client and ways the coach can be even more effective in the future.
  7. Following the completion of the Coach – Client Agreement time period, if there is interest in continuing the relationship a new Coach – Client Agreement is completed and the relationship continues.
  8. A free 9 month follow-up session is offered to the client to evaluate the current state of life at that time.
